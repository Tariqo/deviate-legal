Community Guidelines

Effective July 15, 2026
Deviate is for sharing small, safe moments of curiosity with people you deliberately accept as friends.

Be safe and respectful

Do not create a display name, profile, check-in, or photo that contains or promotes:

  • nudity, pornography, sexual exploitation, or any sexual content involving minors;
  • graphic violence, threats, encouragement of self-harm, or dangerous or illegal acts;
  • hate, harassment, bullying, intimidation, or targeted abuse;
  • private personal information, non-consensual intimate material, or content captured where privacy is reasonably expected;
  • impersonation, fraud, spam, scams, or misleading identity information; or
  • copyrighted or other material you do not have the right to share.

Respect other people

Ask permission before sharing an identifiable image of another person. Never use Deviate to expose someone's location, contact details, confidential information, or private activity. Take particular care with images involving children.

Names and missions

Online display names must pass Deviate's basic safety and impersonation checks. A name that does not pass may still be used for a device-only profile, but it cannot be used online. Online display names can be changed once every 15 days.

Mission completion is honor-based. Deviate does not decide whether a photo proves completion, but every shared item must still follow these Guidelines.

Photo screening

New online photos are automatically screened for sexual, violent, hateful, and self-harm content before appearing to friends. Automated screening can make mistakes and cannot reliably detect every privacy, copyright, or contextual problem. Passing the screen does not mean a photo complies with these Guidelines.

Reporting and blocking

Use Deviate's Report control to report a profile or shared item. You can also remove a friend or block an account. For urgent or follow-up concerns, email tariqali30021@gmail.com.

If someone is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services. Deviate is not an emergency service.

Enforcement

We may remove content, restrict online features, suspend an account, or permanently delete an online account when we reasonably believe these Guidelines have been violated. The response depends on severity, context, repeated conduct, and risks to other people. Serious illegal content may be preserved or reported where the law requires it.

If you believe an enforcement decision was mistaken, contact support with your account handle and enough information to identify the decision. Do not email sensitive images.

Changes

We may update these Guidelines as Deviate develops. The effective date above identifies the current version.
