Support

Help with your account, privacy, safety, or the app.

Contact: tariqali30021@gmail.com
Please include your Deviate handle, device/iOS version, and a short description. Do not email passwords or sensitive photos.

Report a safety problem

For a profile or shared mission, use the in-app Report control so the report includes the identifiers needed for review. You may also block the account immediately. Safety reports are prioritised and we aim to review them within 24 hours.

If someone is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services. Deviate is not an emergency service.

Delete your online account

  1. Open Deviate and sign in to the account.
  2. Open your profile and select Privacy & account.
  3. Select Delete account and confirm.

This deletes hosted account and social data. Your private Field Log is stored on your device and remains there until you remove the app or its local data. If you cannot access the account, email support from the address associated with it.

Turn off online features

You can use Deviate without online social features. Turning them off signs the device out and stops social requests, but does not delete the hosted account. Use Delete account if you want the hosted account removed.

Control online photos

If you do not allow online-photo screening, Friends and other online features remain available: your companion is shown as your avatar, profile photos stay on your device, mission photos stay in your private Field Log, and friends can still see shared mission titles and photo-free check-ins. You can change this choice later under Privacy & account.

Privacy requests

To request access, correction, or deletion of personal information, email support with the subject “Privacy request.” We may need to verify that the account belongs to you before acting.
